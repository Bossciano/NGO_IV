Who We Are

Building Nigerian Futures Through Skills & Community

"Nigeria's greatest resource is not oil — it is her youth. When young Nigerians are skilled and supported, the entire nation rises."

Founded in 2026, Community Youth & Skills Development Society Inc was established with one conviction: every young Nigerian — regardless of background, ability, or circumstance — deserves access to the tools and support needed to build a meaningful future.

We operate across Nigerian communities, delivering hands-on skills training, entrepreneurship support, digital literacy, and mentorship that creates real, lasting change at the grassroots level.

Skills Development Program

Serving Nigerian Youth Since 2026

We work directly with Nigerian youth to unlock potential and create clear pathways into productive, sustainable futures through practical skills and mentorship.

  • Vocational & technical skills training
  • Digital literacy & technology education
  • Entrepreneurship development & mentorship
  • Life skills — leadership, communication, financial literacy
  • Inclusive programs for youth with disabilities
  • Internship, employment & startup connections
  • Agricultural skills & agribusiness development
  • Creative arts, fashion & media production

Vocational & Technical

Hands-on trades training — from electrical work and plumbing to welding, tailoring, and construction — giving youth skills employers need.

Digital & Tech Skills

Computer literacy, coding basics, social media for business, and digital entrepreneurship — equipping youth for Nigeria's growing tech economy.

Entrepreneurship

Business planning, access to micro-funding networks, mentorship from established Nigerian entrepreneurs, and startup launch support.

Why We Exist

The Challenge We're Answering

In Nigeria, millions of young people graduate each year without clear pathways into meaningful work. Youth unemployment remains one of the country's most urgent challenges — and the consequences of inaction are felt in every community.

The answer is not charity — it is skills. Not dependency — it is development. When Nigerian youth are equipped and supported, they don't just survive. They build businesses, lift families, and transform communities.

Community Youth & Skills Development Society Inc exists to be that catalyst. Rooted in Nigeria, committed to her youth, invested in her future.

To develop. To equip. To empower.
